FAQ


How can I order?

DM Our snapchat account (cgslollies) with the items you'd like to order. We'll send you a digital receipt confirming your order and it's price. Please reply to these messages to confirm your order. Once the order is sorted, we pack it and bring it to school where you can receive your goods at the location specified in the receipt.


How do I pay?

Simply bring your money to the collection point and pay us there. Try to bring the exact amount you are owed, as it is difficult for us to give change. We do not accept IOU's and will not let you pay us back later if you're short.


What is your loyalty rewards program?

Every 5 orders, we'll let you order one item for free. We will let you know when you're eligible to receive a free item when you place an order.


You don't have my favourite lollies!

We're incredibly sorry to hear that. If you'd like, you can DM our Snapchat account and request we add lollies you are interested in. We'll certainly take your thoughts into account.


I received an extra item in my order? Is this a mistake?

Nope! We tend to give out free samples every now and then, so if you receive an extra item enjoy it!
